Privacy Policy

Vigrid  ·  Last updated: March 11, 2026

Vigrid is a Norse-mythology strategy game for iOS and Android. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We collect no data. Vigrid does not transmit any information to us or to any third party.

Local Storage

Vigrid stores game statistics and your difficulty preference locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to any other app or service.

  • Game stats — win/loss record, streaks, and play history stored locally only.
  • Preferences — difficulty setting stored in device preferences.

No Network Access

Vigrid makes no network requests of any kind. There is no analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry, and no account system.

Children's Privacy

Vigrid does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue at github.com/needyaz/luci-blue.
